Treasury Governance

PRIVATE FAMILY BANKING operates through constitutional financial discipline rather than emotional financial behavior.

Contribution Law

  • Contributions happen before discretionary spending.
  • Every inflow must be allocated automatically.
  • Liquidity protection takes priority over expansion.

Withdrawal Governance

  • Every withdrawal must strengthen the system.
  • Yellow-class spending requires a 24-hour wait.
  • Red-class spending is automatically denied.

Credit Governance

  • No undocumented loans are permitted.
  • Maximum credit exposure is capped.
  • Repayment visibility is mandatory before approval.
